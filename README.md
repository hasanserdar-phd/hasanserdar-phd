# Dr. Hasan Serdar 🎓

### Asst. Professor of Computer Engineering @ Necmettin Erbakan University

I am a dedicated researcher and educator with a PhD. My work focuses on modern wireless communication technologies, smart systems, and advanced signal processing.

---

### 🔭 Research Interests & Expertise
- **Wireless Communications:** 5G/6G Networks, Cognitive Radio, Spectrum Sensing.
- **Internet of Things (IoT):** Smart Sensors, Energy-Efficient Wireless Sensor Networks.
- **Signal & Image Processing:** Real-time processing, Computer Vision, MOSFET-C Filters.
- **Integrated Circuits:** Low-voltage, low-power analog and digital circuit design.

### 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Languages:** MATLAB, C, C++, Python, Assembly.
- **Tools:** Proteus, Keil, Xilinx Vivado, MATLAB/Simulink.
- **Specialties:** Embedded Systems, Network Protocols, Circuit Simulation.
